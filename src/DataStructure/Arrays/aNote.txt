In case of questions requiring the count of subarrays, prefix sum is used then:
Add to tha map (0,1) mapping
Store the maps with prefix sum and their occurences

Incase of longest subarray with sum K, we use Hashmaps they store the indexes where the prefix sum has occured also don't require (0,1) map
You don't need to update HashMap everytime as for longest subarray we want the prefix sum occurence to be as left as possible

eg: {2,0,0,3}, target=3; the longest subarray will be {0,0,3}, but if map is kept updated the longest subarray will remain 1 as the prefix sum
was 2 till index 2.
